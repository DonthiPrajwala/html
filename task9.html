History of Tanjavur

Thanjavur, also known as Tanjore, has a rich history spanning millennia, primarily shaped by the Chola, Nayak, and Maratha dynasties. It served as the Chola capital from the 9th to the 12th centuries, during which time the city flourished with significant advancements in art, literature, and agriculture..
